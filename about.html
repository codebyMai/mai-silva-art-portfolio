About

Mai Silva is an artist and illustrator living in West Cork, Ireland. Growing up in a family of artist Mai had a chance to experiment with many mediums which she often combines in her art. Mai works mainly with watercolours, coloured pencils and gouache but occassionally ventures into ink, graphite and acrylics. Her works are inspired by nature and invite the viewer to see the world with childlike wonder.
