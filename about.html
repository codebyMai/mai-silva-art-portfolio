About

Mai Silva is an artist and illustrator based in West Cork, Ireland.

Having being born in a family of artist Mai grew up surrounded by art and discovered her passion for painting very early on. She had a chance to experiment with many mediums which she often combines in her art. Mai works mainly with watercolours, coloured pencils, gouache and ink but occassionally ventures into graphite and acrylics. Her joyful, colorful artworks are inspired by nature and invite the viewer to see the world with the childlike wonder.

Process

Every artwork starts with sketches and meticulous planning. Once the sketch is finalized it is being transferes to mounted watercolour paper. Layers of watercolour are applied in stages from light to dark. Once this multi staged and delicate process is finished it is time for detail work with coloured pencils or ink.
