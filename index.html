About

Mai Silva is an artist and illustrator based in West Cork, Ireland.

Having being born in a family of artist, Mai grew up surrounded by art and discovered her passion for painting very early on. She had a chance to experiment with many mediums, which allowed her to develop a unique, mixed-media approach to her art. Mai works mainly with watercolours, coloured pencils, gouache and ink but occassionally ventures into graphite and acrylics. Her joyful, colorful artworks are inspired by nature and invite the viewer to see the world with the childlike wonder. Every painting tells a story.

Process

Each artwork starts with sketches and meticulous planning. Once the sketch is finalized, it is being transferes to mounted watercolour paper. Then follows the slow process of applying subtle layers of watercolour paints. Depending on complexity of the subject it can take hours or days to achieve the desired result. Once this multi staged and delicate process is finished, it is time for detail work with coloured pencils or ink.
